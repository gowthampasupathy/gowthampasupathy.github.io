About Karpagam Colllege Of Engineering

  • College of Engineering is an autonomous institution of the Karpagam Charity Trust established in the year 2000.
  • The college is one of the Karpagam Educational Institutions, affiliated with the Anna University of Chennai and approved by AICTE
  • It is also accredited by NBA, TCS, Microsoft and Wipro.
